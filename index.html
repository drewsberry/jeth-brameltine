The Venue

Our ceremony and reception will be at The Lord Mayor’s Mansion House in Clifton, Bristol.

The address for the venue is:

The Lord Mayor’s Mansion House
Clifton Down
Avon, Bristol
BS8 3LJ

There is free parking available at the Lord Mayor’s Mansion House and on the street outside and the venue is wheelchair accessible.

Please arrive at 13:00 for our 13:30 ceremony.
